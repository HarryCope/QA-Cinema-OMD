About Us

QA Cinema has been a part of Doesnotexistshire's community for the past 10,330 years, and are proud to be a provider of entertainment for all ages.
John Smith founded this cinema while pondering what he was going to have for dinner after a rather intense game of charades. He thought the whole endevour of acting like a fool was absolutely exhausting, and enjoyed watching other people acting like idiots much more. And thus, the cinema was born.
How popcorn was invented (which is a QA Cinema invention) is a story that we can't go into today. Let's just say that it involved a missile silo, a whole lot of corn kernels, and a military grade flamethrower.
Overcharging customers for a large holographic rectangle projected on to a wall made John Smith giddy with joy, so it didn't take long for John to produce 1,200,384 cinemas nationwide, worldwide, planetwide, and even galaxywide.
In fact, John Smith became so powerful that his cinema worker armarda is one of the biggest in the galaxy, and he is known by his subjects as an irritable and ruthless leader. The resistance claims that under John's leadership, countless atrocities have been committed against sentient life across the Milky Way. These disgusting lies are not true, and our glorious leader John is a our supreme overlord that we must be obedient for!
The hotdogs in our cinemas are made of 100% hotdog, and even taste like hotdogs, so you know that you are getting a product that both is made of and tastes like hotdog.
We hope that you enjoy your time at our cinemas! If you do not enjoy your time at our cinemas (which is not possible) you will be tried and executed swiftly by our glorious leader.
In all seriousness, thank you for looking at our website and we hope that you enjoyed using it!
It took a lot of blood, sweat, tears, and coffee (on my part anyway).
We all had our parts to play in this project, with our own individual strengths which we utilised to their full potential.
